For You

I didn’t plan this. Somewhere between the quiet conversations, the late replies, and the way you simply exist — you became something important to me.

So I made something. Just for you.
Your Eyes

There’s something about your eyes that feels calm. They don’t just look beautiful — they feel safe. When you look at the camera, it doesn’t feel posed. It feels honest. And that softness? It stays with me longer than it should.
Your Smile

Your smile changes everything. It’s not loud. It’s not dramatic. It’s warm. The kind of smile that makes ordinary moments feel important. The kind that stays in someone’s mind.
