For You

I didn’t plan this. Somewhere between quiet conversations, late replies, and the way you simply exist — you became something important to me.

So I made something. Just for you.
Your Eyes.

There’s something about your eyes that feels calm. They don’t just look beautiful — they feel safe. There’s a softness in them that makes everything slow down. When you look at the camera, it doesn’t feel posed. It feels real.

Your Smile.

Your smile changes everything. It’s not loud. It’s not dramatic. It’s warm. The kind of smile that stays in someone’s mind. The kind that makes ordinary moments feel special.
