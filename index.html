For You

I didn’t plan this. Somewhere between the quiet conversations, the late replies, and the way you simply exist — you became something important to me.

So I made something. Just for you.

There’s something about your eyes.

They don’t just look beautiful — they feel calm. Like they hold softness without even trying. When you look at the camera, it doesn’t feel posed. It feels real.
And your smile…

It’s not loud. It’s not forced. It’s the kind that slowly grows — and somehow makes everything around it lighter.

You don’t even realize what it does. But I do.
I don’t know when it happened. But I started noticing details. The way you stand. The way you hold yourself. The quiet confidence.

And somewhere in that — I fell for you.
If I’m honest — it was never just about how you look.

It’s the way you make things feel. Softer. Warmer. Real.
