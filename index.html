For You

I didn’t expect you to matter this much. But the way you carry yourself, the way you smile, the way your eyes hold quiet stories — somehow, you became someone I think about more than I planned to.
Your Eyes.

There’s something about your eyes that feels calm. They don’t just look beautiful — they feel safe. There’s a softness in them that makes everything slow down. When you look at the camera, it doesn’t feel posed. It feels real.

Your Smile.

Your smile changes everything. It’s not loud. It’s not dramatic. It’s warm. The kind of smile that stays in someone’s mind. The kind that makes ordinary moments feel important.
